Frontier systems continue to improve quickly, and the gap between leading closed and open-weight ecosystems appears to be narrowing. That supports the scenario’s broad claim that capability progress remains rapid.

The missing evidence is the scenario’s key causal engine: AI systems autonomously producing sustained, compounding gains in frontier AI research. Public demonstrations of coding agents are not the same as reliable research automation. We therefore place reality behind the scenario while keeping the confidence only medium; the most relevant work may be private.
